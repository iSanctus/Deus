Action Prefix
There are 3 types of prefixes for action="" tag:

"Bypass -h" - Use it in Npc Window(it closes page upon click). It can also be used in Community Board, but NOT IN INTERLUDE!

"Bypass" - Use it in Npc Window or Community Board

"link" - Use it in Tutorial Window

Setting bypass/link in action will trigger RequestBypassToServer or RequestTutorialLinkHtml packet upon click.
